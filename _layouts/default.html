I am a second year PhD student at the Computer Science & Engineering department, at the University of Michigan, where I work on structured methods for computer vision and robotics. My PhD advisor is Bernadette Bucher.

I previously worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Visual Concepts Entertainment, developing Machine Learning solutions for the NBA 2K series, focusing on animation, graphics and motion systems.

I have an MSE in Computer & Information Science from the University of Pennsylvania, where I worked with Kostas Daniilidis. I also hold a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering from VIT Vellore, India.
